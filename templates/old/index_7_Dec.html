Who are we?

The Robin Hood Academy empowers 7328+ street children with basic primary education. Our Robins conduct regular weekend classes and excursions with a standardized curriculum to introduce and drive the spirit of learning.The purpose of the Academy is to be a bridge between the street and schools, by giving our children the tools and knowledge to go to school.
